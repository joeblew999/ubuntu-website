## Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

- **Right to Access** - Request information about any data held about you
- **Right to Erasure** - Request deletion of any personal data
- **Right to Rectification** - Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to Object** - Object to processing of your data

Since we do not collect or store personal data, these rights are automatically satisfied.
